About Us

Founded in 1985, the Department of Psychology has been at the forefront of psychological education in the Philippines.

Vision

The department aims to be a leader in the education of outstanding professionals in the fields of counseling psychology and educational psychology in the Asia-Pacific region.

Mission

We offer academic programs emphasizing the integration of theory, research, practice, and service; advancement of culturally sensitive counseling and educational approaches; and promotion of multidisciplinary collaboration.
